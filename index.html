DayZ Epoch

DayZ Epoch is set in a future time after the Great Infection. Humanity is slowly returning to the wasteland, industrious survivors have begun to join together in small groups and rebuild society. Small camps of merchants have started to pop up to do business with survivors who collect goods from the wasted remnants of civilization. Wholesalers have landed on the shores offering imported goods to aid the rebuilding effort.

All is not safe however some vestiges of infection still remain and rogue bandits still roam the countryside preying on unsuspecting survivors.

Survive, explore, build wealth, make your way through the harsh environment and assist in the effort to rebuild, or burn it all down its your choice, live in the Epoch.

Want to be listed as official server?

To add your server to our official servers page you must abide by a few basic rules. Official server admins will receive extra help with server issues from the developers at no cost. Early access to new builds and access to development servers to beta test new features.

1. No extra custom mods, vanilla epoch only.
2. Server Admins may never spawn free gold or vehicles. (except in the case of compensation for reported bugs or server wide events.)
3. Cannot drastically change vanilla epoch settings (i.e. 1000 vehicles)
4. Server admins can enforce and should encourage RP-PVP.
5. No PVP safe zones or trader city protections of any kind.

If you agree, post here to request listing.
